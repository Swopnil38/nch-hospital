Welcome to National City Hospital, a visionary endeavor that was established as a community-initiated public limited company, driven by the passionate business community and dedicated medical practitioners of Chitwan district. Our primary commitment is to ensure the delivery of top-notch, affordable healthcare to those in need. With a robust community of over 200 shareholders, we stand united in our unwavering pursuit of providing high-quality, accessible medical services.

Our journey spans a commendable 15-year history in the healthcare realm, during which we have consistently extended trustworthy care to our patients. Strategically located on Hospital Road in Bharatpur, our institution stands as a testament to our dedication, occupying a sprawling 70,000+ square feet across six stories of our self-owned building, complete with underground parking facilities as well as green premises.

Our range of services encompasses general care, extends to emergency interventions, and delve into the specialized domains of Gastroenterology, Urology, General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Obstetrics and gynaecology, Medical oncology, ENT,Head & neck oncology, Radiology, Modern lab, Histopathogy etc . Within our state-of-the-art infrastructure, we proudly offer 100 inpatient beds, a 12-bed Intensive Care Unit (ICU), a 6-bed Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), four modular operation theaters, a 12-bed Postoperative Ward, and a 10-bed Emergency Ward, all fortified by our skilled and specialized staff.

Beyond our clinical efforts, we are deeply committed to community welfare. Our engagement with health camps and the provision of screening services underscores our dedication to promoting holistic well-being. Additionally, our commitment to fostering healthcare professionals is embodied by our ancillary institution, Shree Medical and Technical College.

At National City Hospital, we stand as a beacon of comprehensive healthcare, driven by our shared mission to make quality medical services accessible to all. Join us in our journey as we continue to set new standards in healthcare excellence.

Message from Chairman.

Welcome to National City Hospital, a distinguished tertiary care general hospital dedicated to delivering affordable and superior healthcare services to the community. Our journey commenced in 2065, when an enthusiastic coalition of business leaders and medical practitioners from the Chitwan district established this venture as a public limited company.

From its inception to the present, our path has seen its share of challenges and successes in the realm of business transactions. Despite this, our unwavering motto remains unrelenting, propelling us towards steadfast growth. Our commitment to excellence is evident in our ownership of a fully equipped hospital facility, meticulously crafted to meet all government standards. Boasting an opulent infrastructure, we offer an expansive 100-bed capacity, inclusive of a 12-bed Intensive Care Unit (ICU), a 12-bed Post Operative Ward (POW), a 6-bed Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), a 5-bed Dialysis Center, and four modular operation rooms. Our medical arsenal further includes a 160-slice CT scanner and a comprehensive Beckman Coulter laboratory setup.

Beyond the confines of our walls, our engagement with the community is integral. We actively partake in various health programs, conduct awareness campaigns, and administer wellness clinics, all contributing to our holistic approach to healthcare. Looking ahead, we envision introducing a health service to home facility, revolutionizing home-based care through services like dressing, physiotherapy, and immunization.

At National City Hospital, our pursuit of excellence knows no bounds. We invite you to join us in our journey of delivering uncompromised, accessible healthcare with compassion and dedication.

- Mr Prachand Lal Pradhan
- Chairman, National City Hospital Ltd.

Message from Managing director.

National City Hospital stands as a premier tertiary care general facility, boasting a comprehensive capacity for medical care. Nestled within the heart of Bharatpur municipality in Nepal's Chitwan district, we extend our services to patients hailing from all corners of the nation. Our establishment operates round-the-clock emergency services, houses 100 inpatient beds, a 12-bed Intensive Care Unit (ICU), four state-of-the-art modular operation theaters, a Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), and oncology day care services.

Our adept care team comprises experts across various specialties, collaboratively providing comprehensive patient care through multidisciplinary team discussions, particularly in complex cases. This approach ensures a seamless flow of patient care. Our ethos extends beyond doctors, embracing robust team dynamics. Our allied health personnel, staff nurses, and auxiliary health workers are all trained meticulously in their respective domains, facilitating the delivery of expert services.

Our commitment to delivering medical information and care with exceptional finesse is unwavering. This dedication is underscored by our investment in electronic medical records and patient care data management systems. This integration allows us to maintain a comprehensive patient history and analyze our practices for continuous enhancement. To remain attuned to the modern generation, we've actively engaged in social media outreach, fostering interactivity and accessibility. Lab reports and investigation findings are conveniently accessible online, and web-based appointments streamline the process.

In conclusion, our pledge is to provide unparalleled quality and affordable medical care through evidence-based practices, ensuring optimal well-being for our valued patients.
